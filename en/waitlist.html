How the Waitlist Works

1. Message us on LINE saying you'd like to join the waitlist (let us know your preferred coat color, sex, and adoption timing). → 2. As soon as the next litter or an opening is confirmed, waitlist members are contacted first. → 3. Visit and reserve.

You can see the kittens currently with us on our Available Kittens page and on Minna no Koneko Breeder.

Join the Waitlist on LINE

As soon as our next litter or adoption schedule is set, we'll announce it here and on LINE. Waitlist members are notified first, before anything goes public.
